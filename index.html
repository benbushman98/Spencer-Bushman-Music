Spencer is a lover of the arts. Sports, literature, and music has driven his life from the very beginning, much due to his parents love of similar passions.

Spencer's father enjoys sports and the pop/rock side of music and his mother enjoys literature, writing and the classical side of music. From a very young age, Spencer's life has been influenced by music.

With a presence in both his junior high and high school choirs, that love of music has grown into a love of piano, which is one of his favorite aspecs of music, to composing and writing his own songs. These passions have fueled his past, the present, and will affect his future. TEST
